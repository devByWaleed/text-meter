About TextMeter App?

Apart from counting words and characters, our online editor offers tools to help you improve your writing. You can change the case of your text. Convert it to UPPERCASE, lowercase, or Capitalize (the first letter of each word) for better readability. Simply place your cursor in the text box above and start typing. You'll see the number of characters and words increase or decrease as you type, delete, and edit them. You can also copy and paste text from another program into the editor above, or use the Copy Text feature to easily copy your text for use elsewhere.

Knowing the word count of a text is often essential. For example, an author might need to meet a minimum or maximum word count for an article, essay, report, story, book, or paper. TextMeter will help ensure your word count reaches a specific requirement or stays within a set limit, all while keeping track of the number of characters.
